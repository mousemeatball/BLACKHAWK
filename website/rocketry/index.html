MCAC + HBRC

How our rocketry division works.

🤝

Community partnership

MCAC's rocketry division operates in close partnership with the Hermosa Beach Rocketry Club, giving students access to mentors, lessons, and step by step guidance through the design process.

🏜️

Real launch days

Hermosa Beach Rocketry students attend launch days in Palm Springs, CA where their work will culminate in a final launch demonstration.

🎓

Mentorship pipeline

HBRC members mentor MCAC students through the entire build process — from OpenRocket simulation to 3d printing to launch day.

Activities

What rocketry members do

🛠️

Build workshops

Weekly meetings Airframe construction, fin alignment, and recovery systems.

📐

Flight simulation

OpenRocket simulations to design and optimize flights before launch day.

🏜️

Launch days

Regular launches in Palm Springs with MCAC mentors

🔬

Electronics

Dual-deployment altimeters, GPS trackers, and custom PCB design teach students real-world skills.
