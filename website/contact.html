❓ FAQ

Do I need experience?

Nope. We teach everything from scratch — rockets, OpenRocket, SimScale, CAD.

Is there a cost?

Membership is free. Competition team members are required to pay dues to support expensive part costs.

What competitions do you enter?

TARC and NRC.

Can I join mid-year?

Yes — new members are welcome any time during the school year for the club team.
